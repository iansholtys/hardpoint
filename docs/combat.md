# Combat

## Combat Types

Tactical - Classic top-down positional-based combat. Typically used for ground-warfare at personal-scale speeds.
Dogfight - Chaotic skirmishes where positions are untracked. Typically used for aerial or space warfare.
Beyond Visual Range - What it says on the tin, indirect combat. Typically used for starting large-scale combats.
Joust - High speed passes with compromised defense which may only last for 1-2 rounds.
Strafing Run - Typically used when smaller vehicles are able to use larger vehicles as environments.

## Combat Phases

Not always, but some combats may have multiple phases to them. For example, there may be 1-2 rounds of Beyond Visual Range combat which is then followed up with a single round of Jousting, after which due to high velocity the combat will end. In this example, it would be known at the start of combat the number of phases and what type they will be in. Another example could be a Dogfight which upon completion leads into a Strafing Run

## Combat Actions

### Full/Break Defense
Full/Break Defense can be declared at any time during the round, even after an enemy attacks as long as the character has not yet taken an action. They get a +2 bonus to their defense but are unable to perform any other actions in combat

### Multiple Actions
A player can take multiple actions in a round, but suffers a penalty to all of their actions and their Defenses equal to the total number of actions being taken
